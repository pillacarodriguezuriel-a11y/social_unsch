# SOCIAL-UNSCH: Academic Social Ecosystem - Project PRD

## 1. Project Overview
**SOCIAL-UNSCH** is a modern, high-fidelity social network exclusively for the university community of the National University of San Cristóbal de Huamanga (UNSCH). The platform integrates academic resources, community engagement, and campus utilities into a single, secure, and gamified ecosystem.

## 2. Design Vision: "Crimson Heritage"
The visual identity is grounded in the institutional prestige of the UNSCH, utilizing a sophisticated palette and professional typography.
- **Primary Color:** Deep Crimson (`#5C0000`) - Represents leadership, action, and institutional identity.
- **Secondary Color:** Muted Crimson (`#A6665C`) - Used for highlights, accents, and interactive elements.
- **Neutral Palette:** Soft Structural Grays (`#847370`) and light surfaces (`#FFF8F6`).
- **Typography:** **Manrope** - A modern sans-serif that balances academic rigor with digital friendliness.

## 3. Core Functional Modules

### A. The Dashboard (Academic Hub)
The central nerve center of the platform.
- **Features:** Real-time campus radar, upcoming events, academic announcements, and quick-access widgets.
- **Navigation:** Persistent left sidebar for seamless cross-module jumping.

### B. Wiki-Banco (Academic Repository)
A peer-to-peer library for sharing exam papers, notes, and study guides.
- **Gamification:** A "Credit Economy" system where users earn credits for approved uploads and spend them on downloads.
- **Empty States:** Highly motivational "hero-centric" designs to encourage first-time contributions in new course directories.

### C. Marketplace Sancristobalino
A trusted student-to-student marketplace.
- **Verification:** Limited strictly to verified `@unsch.edu.pe` accounts.
- **Categories:** Textbooks, electronics, furniture, and academic services.

### D. Conexión Sancristobalina (Matchmaking)
A "Tinder for Thesis" or project partner finder.
- **Functionality:** Match with other students based on academic interests, research goals, or specific faculty needs.
- **Privacy:** Granular controls to hide profiles once a partner is found.

### E. Ruta Sancristobalina (Carpooling)
A safe campus commute network.
- **Verification:** Drivers must have institutional verification checkmarks.
- **Safety:** Real-time seat tracking and route visibility.

## 4. Administrative & Security Suite

### A. Moderación Central
A specialized console for faculty and student moderators.
- **Features:** Auto-suppression of reported content, audit logs with meta-evidence, and permanent banning capabilities.
- **Logic:** Community-driven reporting with automatic concealment after reaching a threshold.

### B. Auth Gateway & Security
- **Domain Locking:** Access is restricted to institutional emails.
- **Privacy Controls:** Centralized ledger for auditing "Academic Credits" and managing campus-wide visibility settings.
- **Session Safety:** Specialized "Secure Logout" screens with reminders for shared library computer safety.

## 5. Technical Requirements
- **Responsive Web:** Optimized primarily for desktop academic use, with mobile-first design considerations for the Radar and Carpooling modules.
- **Interactive States:** Smooth CSS transitions, high-contrast toggle states, and gamified UI feedback (e.g., credit badges).
